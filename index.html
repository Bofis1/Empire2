FEATURES

EVERYTHING BUILT INTO A SINGLE HTML FILE

⚔
Real-Time Combat
Mouse-aimed attacks with a full cooldown system. Combo multipliers at 3×, 6×, 10× hits. Critical strikes trigger screen shake and chromatic aberration. Every hit feels physical.
👑
Multi-Phase Bosses
12 zone bosses with 2–5 escalating phases. New attacks, faster speed, bigger damage at every threshold. The Lich King summons undead adds. Vaeltharax enters a frenzy at 10% HP.
🧬
8 Playable Races
Xu, Human, Gnome, Ogre, Centaur, Minotaur, Elf, Vampire. Each race has unique passives, distinct 3D mesh, and its own stat spread. Combine with any of 4 classes.
⚡
4 Classes · 40+ Skills
Warrior, Mage, Healer, Rogue. Each class has its own skill tree — active attacks, passives, defensive abilities, utility. Spend points on level-up to build your loadout.
🤝
P2P Multiplayer
Host a room, share a 6-character code. Up to 3 friends join browser-to-browser via WebRTC. No server. No account. Host-authoritative enemy sync. Shared boss HP.
🤖
AI Companions
Three AI allies follow you into every run — always one of each missing class so every party has a Warrior, Mage, Healer, and Rogue. Each bot has its own race, name, and pre-game lobby banter. In multiplayer, bots fill any unused slots.
🎒
Deep Loot System
5 rarity tiers. 8 equipment slots. Drag-to-equip with compare tooltips. Gem socketing. 28 crafting recipes across 4 stations. Item vault storage of up to 40 items.
💾
Persistent Saves
Level, stats, skill tree, equipment, vault, gold, quests, and achievements all save to localStorage on every zone transition. Your run survives browser close.
🎵
Dynamic Audio
Per-zone music that crossfades on transition. Zone ambient audio — cave drips, void hum, blizzard. Full SFX: combat hits, critical crunch, boss phase stingers, monster deaths per type.
